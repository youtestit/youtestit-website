Smile

The company Smile is made up of a team of experts on web architectures and open source solutions. With more than 600 employees in the world and recognised open source expertise across a wide variety of areas, Smile is the French leader for the integration of open source solutions.

Our motto: keep it simple, useful and high performing. Actively involved in the development of the Internet since 1995, Smile has produced some of the largest French Internet sites. These websites are of a high value and have large audiences. Smile has also been chosen by some of the largest French companies to produce and maintain strategic Intranet applications serving hundreds of users for thousands of transactions.

Thanks to continued investment in technological monitoring, Smile anticipates trends, and masters products as soon as possible so that they can provide their clients with the most effective and secure open source solutions.
